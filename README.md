# Every 2027 Election

A calendar of every federal, statewide, legislative, judicial and major-local election scheduled for 2027.

**The headline fact:** 2027 is an off-year. There are **no regularly scheduled federal elections** — no
president, no U.S. House, no Senate class. Only special elections can appear, and as of July 2026 none are
scheduled.

## What's in it

- 3 governorships (Kentucky, Louisiana, Mississippi) — two of them open seats
- 19 other statewide executive offices across those three states
- 8 state legislative chambers (LA, MS, NJ, VA — all four run their full legislature)
- 5 statewide judicial seats (Pennsylvania and Wisconsin only)
- 1 certified statewide ballot measure (Louisiana SB 68)
- 32 major local races — big-city mayoralties, prosecutors, and elected election administrators

## Data caveats

- **General election dates are firm.** Primary dates marked "projected" are derived from statutory
  formulas — no state has published an official 2027 election calendar yet.
- **Louisiana votes Nov 20, not Nov 13.** Act 640 of 2024 pushed all 2027 dates one week later. Wikipedia
  currently shows Nov 13, applying the old formula.
- **The local list is curated, not exhaustive.** PA, MS and VA each elect full county slates in 2027 —
  thousands of races. Only the nationally salient ones are included.
